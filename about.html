About Hand Sign Language Detector

Our Mission

Hand Sign Language Detector is dedicated to bridging communication gaps and making sign language more accessible through cutting-edge artificial intelligence. Our mission is to create technology that empowers the deaf and hard of hearing community, making communication more intuitive and inclusive.

Technology

Our advanced machine learning model uses computer vision and deep neural networks to recognize and interpret hand sign language gestures with high accuracy. By analyzing the intricate details of hand positions, we can detect letters, numbers, and potentially even complex sign language phrases.

Impact

We believe that technology can be a powerful tool for inclusivity. By providing an easy-to-use hand sign detection platform, we aim to:

  • Facilitate better communication
  • Support sign language learning
  • Raise awareness about deaf culture
  • Develop assistive technologies
