User Guide: Hand Sign Language Detection

Getting Started

Our Hand Sign Language Detector helps you recognize and understand sign language gestures. Follow these steps to use the application effectively:

  1. Navigate to the home page
  2. Click or drag an image into the upload area
  3. Click "Analyze Image"
  4. View the detected sign and confidence levels

Supported Hand Signs

A: Closed Fist

B: Open Palm

1: Index Finger

2: Two Fingers

3: Three Fingers

4: Four Fingers

Best Practices

  • Use clear, well-lit images
  • Position hand fully within the frame
  • Minimize background distractions
  • Ensure hand is clearly visible

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the Hand Sign Language Detector?

Our AI model is trained on a diverse dataset and can recognize hand signs with up to 95% accuracy under optimal conditions. Accuracy may vary depending on image quality, lighting, and hand positioning.

What types of hand signs can the detector recognize?

Currently, our detector supports recognition of letters A-Z, numbers 0-9, and some basic sign language gestures. We are continuously expanding our recognition capabilities to include more complex signs and phrases.

Is my image data secure?

Yes, we prioritize user privacy. Images are processed securely and are not stored or shared. Our AI analysis is performed anonymously, and no personal information is retained after the detection process.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our Hand Sign Language Detector is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adapts to different screen sizes for optimal user experience.

Do you offer support for learning sign language?

While our primary focus is on sign language detection, we provide resources and guides to help users understand sign language basics. We're committed to making communication more accessible and inclusive.
